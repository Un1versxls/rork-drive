# Skip survey on second paywall close

**Behavior change**

- The first time you close the purchase screen, you'll still see the quick "Why aren't you interested?" survey as usual.
- After you've answered it once, closing the purchase screen again will take you straight back to the previous onboarding page — no survey, no retry paywall.
- This memory lasts for the whole onboarding session, so you won't get asked twice.

No visual changes — just a smoother second close.
